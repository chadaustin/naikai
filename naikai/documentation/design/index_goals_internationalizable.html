Naikai | Goals | Internationalizable

All strings in Naikai will be stored in either UTF-8 or UCS-2 so Naikai will be fully internationalizable. I was really surprised at the amount of people who wished to use non-ASCII characters within their Sphere games, but Sphere only supported ASCII.
